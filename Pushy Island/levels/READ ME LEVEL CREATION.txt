LEVEL CREATION GUIDE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Level Creation is divided into placing the Ground and the Objects.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Level Ground files should be called levelX_ground.txt where X is the number of the level
Level Objects files should be called levelX_objects.txt where X is the number of the level

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Items are placed with indication numbers seperated by space.

For Ground files:
0 = Water
1 = Sand
2 = Gras

For Objects files:
0 = None
1 = Pushy
2 = House
3 = Box
4 = Stone
5 = Palm left
6 = Palm right
7 = Seastar
8 = WaterHole
9 = SandHole
10 = Bottle
11 = Bean
